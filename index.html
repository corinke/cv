Projects

I have worked on a multitude of mediums to make creative pieces, ranging from video to audio to graphic design. I have displayed some of my favourite projects below.

Void - Radio drama

corink · Void

I created this radio drama as a final project in a third year radio module during my degree. It pieced together everything I had learned throughout the duration of my course to work on a solo 10 minute radio drama. I had written a script loosely based on a horror manga Uzumaki by Junji Ito, which was interesting to work with as I transformed a purely visual medium into audio, so I had to reimagine how the scary elements would transfer over. I had also hired actors, created foley by recording household sounds and manipulating them in Adobe Audition, and I edited the audio to the final piece hosted on SoundCloud. I received very positive feedback from my lecturers, where they pointed out it was professional standard.

Volunteering as a Graphic Designer

I worked as a volunteer graphic designer at a local charity, Film Oxford. The brief was to create print leaflets to warn about the dangers of wild swimming, as a counterpart to an educational video, to be distributed around local secondary schools. I had used Adobe InDesign to design the leaflet towards printing specifications, Illustrator to create graphics and used the video to create concise bullet points as the leaflet’s content. I understood the audience was school-aged children so the design was bold, attention-grabbing but not too childish. As a result of this, I received positive feedback from management and I was invited back for more graphics work.
